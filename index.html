Meet the Owner

Built Around Great Food and Community

Hi, I'm Carl Bostick, owner and founder of Bostick's Burgers.

Bostick's Burgers was built around a simple idea: serve fresh, quality beef burgers and crispy fries while providing dependable service that brings people together. Whether we're catering a company lunch, youth sports event, church gathering, or family celebration, my goal is to make every event enjoyable with great food and friendly service.

As a locally owned business, I take pride in every event we cater and appreciate the opportunity to be part of our community's special occasions.
